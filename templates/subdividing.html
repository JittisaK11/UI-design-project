Subdividing

Subdividing is: Breaking the notes in a set of beats such that all notes are expressed in terms of the smallest value present in the set. This gives you a deeper understanding on what notes are played when.

For the basics: We can divide each beat into 2 parts ie eighth notes: up and down beat

Blue lines indicate upbeats, red lines indicate downbeats.

Downbeats and Upbeats

The interplay between downbeats and upbeats helps to create the rhythmic structure of a piece, contributing to its overall feel and flow. While the downbeat often carries a sense of weight or grounding, the upbeat adds movement and anticipation, together shaping the music's rhythm and pace.
