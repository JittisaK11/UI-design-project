Downbeats and Upbeats

The interplay between downbeats and upbeats helps to create the rhythmic structure of a piece, contributing to its overall feel and flow. While the downbeat often carries a sense of weight or grounding, the upbeat adds movement and anticipation, together shaping the music's rhythm and pace. Some songs emphasize the up-beat, while some emphasize the down-beat.
