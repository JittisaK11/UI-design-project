Time Signatures

Compound meters are where each beat can be divided by 3.




How do we interpret this time signature?

The time signature in the image can be interpreted as "six eighth-note beats per hi measure." When hearing music, time signatures can often be interpreted as the ways notes can be heard as a group. The most common ones are 4/4, and 6/8, and 2/4.
